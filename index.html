How we simplify legal matters for you

At the office of Advocate Urmila Nandkumar Jahagirdar, we are dedicated to providing strong legal support with a focus on integrity, expertise, and personalized guidance. With years of experience, we assist individuals, families, and organizations in navigating complex legal matters across a wide range of practice areas:
Family Law, Civil Cases, Co-operative Cases, School Tribunal Cases, Trust & Charity Commissioner Cases.
We aim to simplify the legal process for our clients, offering clear solutions, strong advocacy, and dependable representation in every case.

Family Cases

Compassionate guidance in matters related to marriage, divorce, custody, maintenance, and family disputes.

Civil Cases

Expert representation in property disputes, contracts, recovery matters, and civil litigation.

Trust Cases

Specialized services in trust registrations, management, compliance, and disputes before the Charity Commissioner.

Co-operative Cases

Assistance with disputes and compliance related to housing societies, co-operative institutions, and their regulations.

Consumer Cases

Legal assistance for protecting consumer rights, resolving disputes, and seeking fair compensation.

School Tribunal

Legal support for teachers, staff, and institutions in tribunal-related issues.
